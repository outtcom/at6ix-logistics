Our Services

End-to-end delivery and logistics solutions for businesses and residential clients across Ontario — from Toronto to the province's edge.

01

Appliance Delivery & Installation

AT6ix handles the full cycle of appliance logistics — pickup from supplier or warehouse, careful transport across Ontario, and in-home placement or installation at the customer's residence or job site. Our teams are trained to manage large, heavy items including refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, and dishwashers with precision and care. We protect flooring, navigate tight spaces, and leave the site clean.

GTA & Province-Wide
02

White Glove Delivery Services

For fragile, high-value, and oversized items that demand premium care, AT6ix's White Glove service goes far beyond standard delivery. We provide careful indoor placement, assembly where required, full debris and packaging removal, and climate-aware transport to protect sensitive items in transit. Ideal for luxury furniture, fine art, high-end electronics, large appliances, and custom commercial installations.

Premium Service
03

Drop & Go Deliveries

When volume and speed matter most, AT6ix's Drop & Go service delivers. Designed for high-volume commercial routes, this efficient curbside or lobby delivery model eliminates wait times and maximises driver productivity. Businesses relying on rapid replenishment — retailers, offices, warehouses, and distribution centres — rely on Drop & Go for fast, predictable turnaround without compromising reliability.

High-Volume Commercial
04

Last-Mile Delivery

The final leg from warehouse or distribution centre to the end customer is the most complex and costly part of the supply chain — and the most visible to your customers. AT6ix's last-mile delivery operation is real-time tracked, route-optimised, and built for commercial volume across all of Ontario. We integrate with your dispatch systems and deliver on your brand promise, every time.

Real-Time Tracked

Scheduled & On-Demand Transport

AT6ix gives clients the flexibility to choose how they operate. Set up recurring scheduled routes for predictable, high-frequency deliveries, or leverage on-demand dispatch for variable needs. Whether you're running daily retail replenishment routes or need a single-day urgent pickup, our booking system and dispatch team accommodate any volume and any timeline.

Flexible Booking

Services FAQ

Q What delivery services does AT6ix offer?

AT6ix Logistics offers appliance delivery and installation, white glove delivery, drop and go curbside delivery, last-mile delivery, scheduled and on-demand transport, and both commercial and residential deliveries across Ontario.

Q Does AT6ix do appliance delivery in Toronto?

Yes. AT6ix provides appliance delivery and installation across the GTA — Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Scarborough — and province-wide across Ontario. We handle pickup, transport, and in-home placement or installation of all major appliances.

Q What is the difference between White Glove and Drop & Go delivery?

White Glove is AT6ix's premium in-home service for fragile, high-value, or oversized items — including indoor placement, assembly, and debris removal. Drop & Go is a fast curbside or lobby delivery model for high-volume commercial routes where speed is the priority.

Q Does AT6ix do residential deliveries?

Yes. AT6ix serves both residential and commercial clients across Ontario. Residential services include home appliance delivery and installation, white glove in-home placement, and standard parcel delivery across the GTA and province-wide.

Q How do I book a scheduled delivery?

Fill out the quote form at at6ixlogistics.com/#quote or email info@at6ixlogistics.com. AT6ix offers both recurring scheduled routes and on-demand dispatch. All enquiries receive a response within 2 business hours, Monday through Friday.
