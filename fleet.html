Our Fleet

Box Trucks

Enclosed Transport

AT6ix's box truck fleet is the backbone of our appliance delivery, furniture transport, and commercial freight operations. Enclosed on all sides, box trucks protect cargo from weather, road debris, and theft — essential when moving high-value or fragile goods across Ontario. Our drivers are trained in load securing, furniture blanket wrapping, and appliance handling techniques to ensure every item arrives in the condition it left.

Appliance Delivery Furniture Transport Commercial Freight Retail Replenishment
02

Sprinter Vans

Urban & White Glove

Sprinter vans are AT6ix's preferred vehicle for white glove deliveries, time-sensitive urban routes, and premium client-facing services across the GTA. Their height, cargo volume, and manoeuvrability make them ideal for navigating downtown Toronto, Mississauga, and Brampton while still carrying significant freight. Sprinters combine the professionalism and presentation that premium deliveries demand with the agility needed for high-density urban environments.

White Glove Delivery Urban Routes Time-Sensitive Freight GTA Coverage
03

Cargo Vans

Last-Mile & Parcel

For high-volume parcel runs and last-mile delivery operations across the GTA and Ontario, cargo vans provide the efficiency and capacity that courier-scale operations demand. AT6ix deploys cargo vans on dense residential and commercial routes where speed, stop density, and fuel efficiency are critical. Whether supporting e-commerce fulfilment operations or running commercial drop-and-go routes, cargo vans are the engine of AT6ix's daily delivery volume.

Last-Mile Delivery Parcel Delivery E-Commerce Fulfillment Drop & Go
04

Flatbeds

Industrial & Oversized

When freight is too large, too heavy, or irregularly shaped for enclosed transport, AT6ix's flatbed trucks handle the job. Flatbeds are essential for construction materials including lumber, drywall, and steel, as well as industrial components, heavy machinery, and skidded freight that requires forklift loading. Our flatbed operators are experienced in load strapping, securement compliance, and safe transport of oversized freight to job sites and industrial facilities across Ontario.

Construction Materials Steel & Industrial Oversized Freight Skidded Loads
05

Tractor-Trailers

Long-Haul & Heavy Freight

AT6ix's tractor-trailer fleet handles province-wide pallet runs, heavy commercial freight, and high-volume B2B loads. Extending our reach beyond the GTA, tractor-trailers serve clients who need full truckload (FTL) capacity across Ontario — from Toronto distribution centres to northern and eastern destinations. Ideal for retail chain replenishment, manufacturing supply runs, and large-scale construction materials delivery.

Full Truckload (FTL) Long-Haul Ontario Heavy Freight Pallet Runs

Fleet Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Q What vehicles does AT6ix use?

AT6ix operates a versatile fleet including box trucks for large appliance and commercial freight, Sprinter vans for white glove and time-sensitive urban deliveries, cargo vans for high-volume parcel and last-mile delivery, flatbed trucks for construction materials and industrial freight, and specialized equipment for unique cargo needs.

Q Does AT6ix have box trucks for appliance delivery?

Yes. AT6ix's box truck fleet is purpose-built for appliance delivery across Ontario. Enclosed on all sides, they protect refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, and other large appliances from weather and damage during transport, with trained operators who handle in-home placement and installation.

Q What is a Sprinter van used for?

AT6ix uses Sprinter vans for white glove deliveries, time-sensitive freight, and urban route deliveries across the GTA. Sprinters are versatile — they navigate city streets efficiently, carry significant cargo volume, and present the professional image expected for premium delivery services.

Q Does AT6ix have flatbed trucks?

Yes. AT6ix operates flatbed trucks for construction materials, steel, pipes, lumber, drywall, oversized industrial items, and skidded freight across Ontario. Our flatbed operators are trained in load securement and safe delivery to job sites and industrial facilities.
