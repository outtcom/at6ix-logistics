Frequently Asked
Questions

Q What delivery services does AT6ix offer?

AT6ix offers appliance delivery and installation, white glove delivery, drop and go deliveries, last-mile delivery, scheduled and on-demand transport, and commercial and residential deliveries across all of Ontario.

Q Does AT6ix deliver and install appliances in Toronto?

Yes. AT6ix provides appliance delivery and installation across the GTA and all of Ontario — refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, and dishwashers handled with care, including in-home placement and installation.

Q What is White Glove Delivery?

White Glove Delivery is AT6ix's premium service for fragile, high-value, or oversized items — careful transport, indoor placement, assembly where required, debris removal, and climate-aware handling.

Q What types of freight does AT6ix transport?

AT6ix transports skidded freight, appliances, construction materials, steel and industrial materials, and windows and fragile building supplies using a versatile fleet of box trucks, Sprinter vans, cargo vans, and flatbeds.

Q What vehicles does AT6ix operate?

AT6ix operates box trucks, Sprinter vans, cargo vans, flatbeds, and specialized equipment — a versatile fleet built to handle jobs of all sizes across Ontario.
