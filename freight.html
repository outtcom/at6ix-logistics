Common Questions

Freight FAQs

What types of freight does AT6ix handle?

AT6ix handles skidded freight, household and commercial appliances, construction materials (drywall, lumber, concrete board), steel and industrial materials (beams, pipes, rebar, metal studs), and windows and fragile building supplies. Our fleet and handling expertise are matched to each freight type across Ontario.

Can AT6ix deliver construction materials to job sites in Ontario?

Yes. AT6ix regularly delivers construction materials including drywall, lumber, plywood, concrete board, insulation, and structural components to active job sites across the GTA and province-wide Ontario. Our flatbed fleet and experienced drivers handle job-site logistics safely.

Does AT6ix handle steel and industrial freight?

Yes. AT6ix transports steel beams, pipes, rebar, metal studs, and other heavy industrial materials. Our flatbed trucks are equipped with chains, binders, and tie-down equipment for heavy and oversized industrial freight on Ontario routes.

Can AT6ix deliver windows and fragile building materials?

Yes. AT6ix specialises in fragile building supply logistics including windows, glass panels, glazing units, ceramic tiles, and other breakage-sensitive building materials. We use vertical A-frame racking, padding, and experienced handling to ensure safe delivery to job sites and showrooms across Ontario.

How do I get a freight quote from AT6ix?

Submit the quote form at at6ixlogistics.com/#quote or email info@at6ixlogistics.com with your freight type, weight, dimensions, pickup location, and destination. AT6ix responds to all freight quote requests within 2 business hours, Monday through Friday.
